We are now open for public services every Sunday morning, starting 10.30am and finishing 11.30am. You are welcome to join us.

There is a 'first come – first served' policy in operation, with a maximum of 30 people permitted in the church building. Please wear a mask, and wait in the foyer for someone to offer you hand sanitizer and take you to a socially-distanced seat.

For more information, visit the Notices section of the site.
