In view of the latest advice from the Government, we have made the decision to suspend all church-related meetings and events – Sunday services, clubs, and housegroups – for the foreseeable future.

This does not stop us being a church – members of the body of Christ scattered into the world! This time has provided new ways of relating to and caring for each other, new modes of learning together, and new opportunities to witness to Jesus and the hope he gives us.

For more information, visit the Newsletter section of the site.
